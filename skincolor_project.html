The Skin Color Project End Product

The Skin Color Project was where we learned about skin color in five of our classes (STEM Foundations, Science, Language Arts, Honors Algebra, and US History). Our goal was to answer the question “How and why does skin color matter?” We answered these questions through an end product we made in STEM Foundations. Some of our choices were, an infographic, PSA, and whiteboard video (what my partner and I did). We created these end products solely with Adobe products like Premier Pro, After Effects, and Illustrator. This end product was to sharpen our skills with Adobe products. Our end products were supposed to go to museums and front desks at universities.
