Introduction

Hello! My name is Nathan Varghese and you are looking at my very own Dayton Regional STEM School (DRSS) portfolio; coded with HTML (Hyper Text Mark-up Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and some JavaScript. Coding my portfolio began 6 years ago in 6th grade and each year I developed my coding skills to create a digital portfolio tailored to my interests. I am now a graduating senior at DRSS and plan on going to The Ohio State University for chemical engineering. You can find highlights, or my favorite projects I reflected on, under the Projects dropdown, dating all the way back to 7th grade! Some of the pages you can find on my website include my Experiences, which you will learn more about me; my Coding Progression page, highlighting how I have grown in my coding skills each year; and my 5 Qualities page, highlighting the five major qualities that make a STEM student unique and my definition and experience with each of them. I hope you enjoy scrolling through my portfolio and can see for yourself how I have grown in coding, language/writing, and professionalism in my 6th-12th educational career at DRSS.
